What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are used to store small pieces of user data. The cookies are stored on your device when the website is loaded on your browser. The cookies help make the website function properly. Cookies make the website more secure while providing a better user experience. Developers use cookies to understand how the website is performing. Along with analyzing what works, what doesn't work and where improvements are needed.

How does {{thisSite}} use cookies?

As with most of the online websites and services {{thisSite}} uses first-party in tandem with third-party cookies for a wide range of purposes. All purposes collectively used for catering to a better and more personalized online user experience.

The first-party cookies used on {{thisSite}} are largely necessary for the website to function the properly intended way, while potentially collecting some of your personally identifiable data depending on the user's case.

The third-party cookies used on {{thisSite}} are used primarily for measuring the performance of the website. Data collection on how you interact with our website. Keeping our services secure and providing advertisements that are relevant to you. The mechanism of which provides you with a better and improved user experience with collected analytical data. Along with helping speed up your future interactions with our website.
